ClawMe Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Overview

ClawMe ("we", "us") is an open-source browser extension and service that bridges AI Agents and users. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data ClawMe collects, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Extension (Browser)

  • Backend URL and Token: Stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device. Never transmitted to us.
  • Instructions: Fetched from your configured backend server. Instructions are between your AI Agent and your browser — we do not see or store them.
  • Execution logs: Stored locally in your browser. Not sent anywhere.

2.2 Backend Server

  • Instructions: Stored in memory only. No database. Instructions are automatically cleared when the server restarts.
  • Tokens: Used for authentication. Configured by the server operator (you).
  • No user accounts: ClawMe does not require registration or personal information.

2.3 Website (clawme.net)

  • The website is static and does not collect any personal data.
  • No cookies, no analytics, no tracking.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No browsing history
  • No page content (unless you explicitly use the "extract" instruction)
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No third-party tracking

4. How Data Is Used

All data stays between your AI Agent, your self-hosted backend, and your browser. ClawMe acts as a relay — it does not analyze, monetize, or share any data.

5. Self-Hosted Architecture

ClawMe is designed to be self-hosted. You run your own backend server, which means:

  • Your instructions never pass through our servers
  • You control all data
  • You can audit the open-source code at GitHub

6. Permissions Explained

storageSave your backend URL and token locally
scriptingExecute instructions (fill forms, click elements, extract content) on web pages — only when you click "Execute"
sidePanelShow the ClawMe side panel UI
alarmsPeriodic polling for new instructions from your backend
notificationsDesktop notifications when new instructions arrive
tabsOpen new tabs and navigate for instruction execution

7. Third-Party Services

ClawMe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

8. Children's Privacy

ClawMe is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub or email: privacy@clawme.net
